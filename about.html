About Jon

Writing an 'About Me' section is difficult. Do I write it in first person or should Jonathan write in a more formal manner? Here's my attempt at finding the perfect middle ground.

I'm 19 years old and I'm studying Computer Systems at Heriot Watt in Edinburgh, UK. I absolutely enjoy what I do and what I'm studying*, albeit not all of it(my friends may attest to this). Do feel free to click around, I'm sure you'll stumble upon my portfolio or my Github page somehow.

I am also a self proclaimed hobbyist photo- and videographer. On the summer before moving to university, I decided to undertake a project which involved producing weekly content throughout that summer. It was this project that made me realize how fun photo- and videography can be.
